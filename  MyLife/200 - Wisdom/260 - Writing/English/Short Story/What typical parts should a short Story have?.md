
Every well crafted short story includes nine essential parts, which play a distinct role in shaping the impact of the story:

## Exposition

A good Exposition not only introduces the main characters. setting and initial situation but also sets the tone and gives readers context, which they need to understand what is coming next.

## Inciting Incident

This is the moment which disrupts the current status and reveals the central conflict. Its the spark which drives the plot forward.


## Rising Action

The Rising Action is a series of events which build tension and deepen the created conflict. Characters start facing obstacles, making decisions and revealing their motivation.


## Climax

The Climax is the turning point, a moment of high intensity, in which the protagonist confronts the core challenge, often risking everything.


## Falling Action

The Aftermath of the Climax, which shows how characters and the "world" responds to the just created turning point.

## Resolution

The conflict is resolved and every loose end is tied up. It leaves the reader with a sense of closure.


-----


## Characters

Every character should be well-defined, even in a short story. You can achieve this by using dialogue, action and internal thoughts to reveal the depths of the character. To avoid overcrowding focus on one or two central figures.


## Setting

Choose Time and place wisely, they should enhance the mood and also support the plot by feeling real and relevant.

## Theme

The Theme is the message or idea, which is often revealed trough characters choices and consequences
